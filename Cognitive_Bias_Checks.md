# Cognitive Bias Mitigation Checklist
## Structured Analytic Technique (SAT) for Stylometric Operations

Human analysts routinely project personal expectations onto targeted profiles. Apply this check list during analytical derivation to preserve scientific objectivity and maintain Advanced Analytic Dominance.

### 1. Confirmation Bias Mitigation
*   [ ] Did you search exclusively for linguistic patterns that fit your initial attribution theory?
*   [ ] Have you collected a baseline corpus of the target's verified communications to compare against current data?

### 2. Mirror Imaging Prevention
*   [ ] Are you assuming the adversary writes, thinks, or constructs sentences like a Western analyst?
*   [ ] Have you factored in linguistic shifts caused by formal translation, localized slang, or cultural idioms via the `Cultural_Nexus_Mapping.json`?

### 3. Over-Interpretation of Randomness (Clustering Illusion)
*   [ ] Does the detected rhythmic pattern repeat across multiple documents, or does it exist uniquely in a single sample?
*   [ ] Is the calculated $TTR$ deviation statistically significant ($> 10\%$ shift from baseline)?

### 4. Anchoring Defense
*   [ ] Are you relying entirely on the first stylometric report generated, or are you continuously updating the profile with fresh intelligence inputs?
